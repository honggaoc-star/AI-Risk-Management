# Research Streams

This directory contains focused research programs developed within AI Risk Management.

Each research stream should define a bounded problem, its relationship to the broader program, the relevant literature, a proposed design or analytical framework, and an evaluation strategy.

## Active Stream

- [Objective and Design Drift](./Objective-and-Design-Drift/) — investigates unacknowledged divergence from user-authorized project state during extended human–AI collaboration and the feasibility of a specialized, low-friction monitoring model.

Additional streams should be added only when they have a distinct research question and sufficient conceptual development.
